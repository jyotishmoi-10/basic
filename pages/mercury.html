Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet in the Solar System and closest to the Sun. It has a very thin atmosphere and experiences extreme temperature variations. During the day, temperatures can reach up to 430째C (800째F), while at night, they can drop to -180째C (-290째F).

Mercury has a large iron core that makes up about 75% of the planet's diameter. This core is partly responsible for the planet's weak magnetic field. Mercury's surface is covered with craters, similar to the Moon, and it has no moons of its own.

Because Mercury is so close to the Sun, it orbits the Sun in just 88 Earth days. This rapid orbit and its slow rotation mean that a single day on Mercury (sunrise to sunrise) lasts 176 Earth days.
